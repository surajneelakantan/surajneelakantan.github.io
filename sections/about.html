About

I am an Industrial PhD candidate in Computer Science at Örebro University, working in collaboration with Orexplore AB — a company pioneering geoscience sensing technology. My research applies deep learning to the unique challenges of geological data: limited labels, noisy signals, and multi-modal sensor fusion.

My thesis, Learning Beneath the Surface, presents a unified framework built around three core themes: domain adaptation, multi-modal integration, and interpretable image super-resolution for geoscience and remote sensing applications.

Beyond research, I co-developed the Neural Networks course at Örebro University and am passionate about making deep learning interpretable and controllable for domain experts in geology and mining.
